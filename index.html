How to calculate NU CGPA?

Calculating your National University (NU) CGPA manually is difficult. Our tool automates the process:

1. Download your result PDF from the official NU result website.
2. Upload the file here.
3. Our AI parser reads the "Course Code", "Credit", and "Letter Grade".
4. We calculate the GPA based on the official NU grading scale.

NU Grading Scale

80% - 100%: A+ (4.00)

75% - 79%: A (3.75)

70% - 74%: A- (3.50)

65% - 69%: B+ (3.25)

60% - 64%: B (3.00)

00% - 39%: F (0.00)
